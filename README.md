## April 13th, 2026
My family has an unfinished basement that my Dad's interested in developing. As the floor plan changed from the original design, I was tasked with translating his new plan into the current plans I have made today. He needs the plans in order to work easily whenever he gets to wiring. I measured the dimensions of each room, then created a geometric representation with the data I measured into AutoCAD. I will add real images in order to show what I was doing, but as of now, I have a decent representation of the unfinished basement. One thing to note is that I have a flaw in measuring the dimensions: the wall thickness. In reality, there is a hidden 4.5" wall in between each line separating the Mechanical, Bedroom and the Bathroom. That is one thing I could improve in dimensioning things properly.
